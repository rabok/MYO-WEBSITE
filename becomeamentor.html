Become a Mentor

Mentoring is one of the most rewarding ways that make a lasting Impact on anyone's life. As a mentor, you provide the youth with an opportunity to develop skills, and receive guidance and much-needed support that they would otherwise not have.

Start Mentoring!

Get started!

1. Signup below and we will revert with a phone call

2. Send us the mentor application form and we will match you with a mentee

3. Recieve an email and introduce you to your mentee

Good to know - Information for mentors

The mentor

A mentor will help his/her mentee have hope in life, develop leadership skills, explore career paths, bridge the gap between parents/teachers and the child, and subsequently become change makers and mentor the next generation. The ultimate goal is to have a generation of confident people who are active contributors to the society.

Mentor Qualifications

Maisha Yangu Organization is looking for committed mentors who champion for children and youth empowerment.

To be eligible, you must at least have one of the following:

Finished your higher learning or enrolled in university/college pursuing a degree from any field of study.

Have transferable skills.

Experience in various professions.

The Mentees

The mentees will be from schools in Kawangware and in grade 6, 7 and 8 for primary and all classes in secondary school.

Meeting Frequency

The mentorship meetings take place at Maisha Yangu centre once a week for 2-3 hours and virtually for 30 minutes to 2 hours..
