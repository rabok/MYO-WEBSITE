Become a Distance Parent

Maisha Yangu's distance parenting program enables you to support children, their families and entire communities - giving them the chance to grow up healthy, educated and safe - forever changing their life and future. The program is not just about financial assistance; it's about building meaningful connections, nurturing dreams, and fostering a sense of belonging. Join us in making a difference in a child's life.

Good to know - Information for distance parents

For what you receive

By joining the distance parenting program, you become part of Maisha Yangu Organization's donor community network. You will receive progress reports of the grades and seasonal greetings from your supported student at the end of each school term. In addition, you will be able to communicate with your supported student through video calls, emails, letters and drawings on a regular basis, as well as physical meetups if possible. This communication is an essential part of the program, enabling you to hear from the students and create a relationship with them and their families. We put emphasis on partnering with the parents/ caregivers in providing for the students to foster a sense of responsibility and involvement in the children's lives.

What child recieves

With your financial support of as little as 35 dollars per month depending on the package chosen, we can provide the children with school fees, school materials, a hot meal a day and a safe and friendly place to learn and play.

Students

Our students come from Kawangware and are from families living in poverty. Children supported are in grade 4 to 8 and all classes in secondary school.

Duration

The minimum duration for the program is one term , which is 3 months. Note that one time donations are accepted but monthly donations are encouraged.
